Hello, I’m Kareem Ahmed — a computer science student passionate about programming, problem solving, and creating meaningful digital experiences. My interests span web development, game development, and competitive programming.

I believe in learning through building. I started with small front-end projects, then moved on to frameworks like React. I’ve also taken part in group work, including Trio Web Projects, exploring ideas with friends.

My strongest foundation is in C++, where I learned core programming concepts such as data structures & algorithms, pointers, and OOP — skills I’ve applied in projects like a terminal-based platformer. I’ve also explored Unity with small games such as Pong 3D and Space Game, and more recently began learning backend development through PHP.

Outside of building projects, I sharpen my problem-solving skills through competitions like the ECPC and regular practice on Codeforces.

I aim to create software that blends creativity, usability, and technical precision — whether it’s a web interface, a game, or an algorithmic solution.
